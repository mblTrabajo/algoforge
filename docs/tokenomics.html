Post-Mint Sustainability Steps

  • 🔥 Deflationary model: A portion of every fee and bot commission is permanently burned to reduce supply and boost long-term scarcity.
  • 🔒 Locked liquidity: The core liquidity pool will be locked for over 3 years, protecting against rug pulls and early manipulation.
  • 🔁 Bot commission rebuy: Fees generated by bots are recycled to buy back $$$$ tokens and burn them, fueling a self-sustaining loop.

  • 📈 Auto-scaled bot limits: As more users join, bot capacity scales proportionally based on tier holding, preventing congestion.
